why I start this blog？

Posted on 2020-03-21

This blog is built with both Github Pages Service and Hexo framework. In the process of building the basic framework of this blog website, I got help from Youtuber ‘codesheep’ and all his video tutorials. I would thank the author for his sharing of knowledges.

As a student majoring in Mathematics in undergrad and Financial Mathematics for grad degree, I can clearly feel the trend in the job market nowadays. Coding is becoming an almost necessary for all STEM major students to get a job. Both Tech companies and non-Tech companies are opening huge amount of positions for students who have CS or DS backgrounds.

For the master’s degree, the coding is also becoming more and more desired by employers. Since 2015, many universities start to offer master program in Business Analytics(MSBA). This program is like a combination of CS, DS and business.

For me, learning coding and Data Science is not only a way to become more competitive in the job market, but also a skill that can help me do a lot of cool things. With a laptop I can do almost anything with coding. That makes me want to have a try in getting deeper in coding.

I know that I started relatively late in this study field, but I believe there is nothing difficult if I can keep paying effort to it.

Also, the blog posts will be in either English or Chinese, depending on which one I choose to use.
